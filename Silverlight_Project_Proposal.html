AI Brain - Client Communication & Retention System

An intelligent multi-channel communication system designed to maintain constant engagement with all clients and contacts.

Build a communication system that replies instantly and personally to every client or contact who writes to us on email, WhatsApp, or LinkedIn. Keeps full awareness of every previous interaction, across all channels.

Core Components:

1. LinkedIn & Company Extraction

▼
When a form is submitted with a company LinkedIn URL, the system reviews the company's page and gathers contacts who hold relevant job titles.

Technical Requirements:

  • LinkedIn profile enrichment (phone/email extraction)
  • Company-wide contact discovery
  • Automated email and WhatsApp outreach sequences
  • Quarterly re-engagement campaigns

CRITICAL RISK LinkedIn does not provide API access for automated scraping or messaging. Web scraping violates LinkedIn Terms of Service and can result in account bans and legal action.

2. Scheduling & Rescheduling Automation

▼
Make it effortless to arrange, confirm, or reschedule introductory or follow-up calls with clients. The system detects a client's timezone, speaks naturally on both email and WhatsApp, creates and edits calendar invites automatically.

Features:

  • Automatic timezone detection and conversion
  • Multi-channel scheduling (email + WhatsApp)
  • Calendar integration (Google Calendar, Office 365)
  • Intelligent rescheduling handling
  • No-show follow-up automation

Estimated Time: 60-80 hours

3. Form-Driven Follow-Ups

▼
Ensure every client who has received or submitted a form (for access, onboarding, or setup) is reminded until it's completed. All reminders go by email and WhatsApp in a friendly cadence.

Coverage:

  • 21 different form types tracked
  • Automated reminder sequences (email → WhatsApp → follow-ups)
  • Deferral memory and automatic restart
  • Completion tracking and notifications

4. AI Intelligent Follow-Up System

▼
Every contact we've interacted with must stay in regular, meaningful communication through email, WhatsApp, or LinkedIn. Every message feels personal, relevant, and connected to their history with us.

AI Capabilities:

  • Context-aware message generation (reads full email/WhatsApp history)
  • Adaptive follow-up timing based on response patterns
  • Sentiment analysis and churn detection
  • 30-day engagement guarantee for all contacts
  • Automatic prioritization and daily call lists

Estimated Time: 120-160 hours

5. LinkedIn Monitoring & Engagement

▼
Stay present in every client's professional life by noticing their activity on LinkedIn. The AI Brain congratulates, comments, and reconnects whenever a client gets promoted, changes company, or publishes a post.

LEGAL RISK Automated LinkedIn monitoring and messaging is prohibited by LinkedIn's Terms of Service.

Alternative Approach: Manual LinkedIn monitoring with AI-assisted message drafting (semi-automated workflow)

Project Intake, Expert Sourcing & Auto-booking System

End-to-end automation for managing expert consulting projects, from initial client request to completed calls and payments.

The Silverlight Expert Autobooking & Scheduling System automates the full life cycle: Project Intake → Expert Sourcing → Project Updates → Invitations & Chasing → Call Scheduling → Pre-/During-/Post-Call → Payment → Recordkeeping → AI followups.

Core Workflow:

1. Project Intake

▼
Client emails initiate the process. The bot checks if there is a live project for this person, analyzes the email to see if it is a request for experts, and ensures all required briefing elements are gathered.

Requirements Extracted:

  • Relevant companies/experience
  • Current vs former employees
  • Geography/countries
  • Key themes and job titles
  • Screening questions

AI Processing: NLP to parse unstructured client emails and extract structured project requirements

Estimated Time: 30-40 hours

2. Expert Sourcing & Matching

▼
As soon as the project is created, the system searches our existing network for relevant experts. If five or more relevant profiles are found, immediately email these profiles to the client.

AI Matching Algorithm:

  • Semantic search across expert database
  • Compatibility scoring (past roles vs project needs)
  • Industry side categorization (producers/suppliers/clients)
  • Automatic expert ranking and explanation

Estimated Time: 40-60 hours

3. Expert Invitation & Response Handling

▼
Contact expert(s) via email, WhatsApp, and AI caller. Confirm availability and answers to screening questions. Once all info is complete, provide to the client and initiate call scheduling.

Multi-Channel Chase Sequence:

  • Email → 1 hour → WhatsApp → 1 hour → AI call
  • 3 full chase cycles over 7 days
  • Automated screening question collection
  • Timezone-aware quiet hours (no messages 9pm-7am)

COST CONSIDERATION AI voice calls cost approximately $0.10-0.50 per minute

4. Call Scheduling Automation

▼
Once expert responses and client approvals are received, the booking sequence begins automatically. The system must handle every possible combination of known and unknown availabilities.

Features:

  • Automatic timezone conversion and reconciliation
  • Zoom meeting creation with unique links
  • Office 365 calendar invites for both parties
  • 24h and 1h automated reminders (email + WhatsApp)
  • Rescheduling and cancellation handling
  • Multi-expert booking coordination

Estimated Time: 80-120 hours

5. AI Co-Pilot (During Call)

▼
If both sides consent, the AI Co-Pilot silently joins: Live-transcribes the discussion, generates structured notes, suggests real-time prompts to the client, tags themes for analytics and indexing.

Capabilities:

  • Real-time speech-to-text transcription (Whisper/Deepgram)
  • Live prompt suggestions to client
  • Post-call summary generation within 10 minutes
  • Theme tagging and knowledge graph integration

Estimated Time: 80-100 hours

6. Payment Automation

▼
After a call is completed, Zoom duration data is collected. Client receives email to confirm actual duration. If expert agrees, automatic payment draft in Veem for manual approval.

Workflow:

  • Automatic fee calculation based on client domain + expert rate
  • Duration confirmation from both parties
  • Veem API integration for payment processing
  • Revenue tracking and audit logging

Estimated Time: 60-80 hours

⚠️ Critical Risks & Challenges

1. LinkedIn Automation - Legally Impossible

LinkedIn's API does not provide functionality for automated messaging or profile scraping. Web scraping violates LinkedIn Terms of Service and can result in:

  • Permanent account bans
  • Legal action from LinkedIn
  • Reputation damage

Recommendation: Remove LinkedIn automation or replace with manual process assisted by AI-drafted messages

2. Legal Compliance (GDPR, TCPA, CAN-SPAM)

Automated communications with personal data storage and outreach require:

  • GDPR compliance for EU contacts (consent, data retention, right to deletion)
  • TCPA compliance for automated calls/SMS in USA
  • CAN-SPAM compliance for email marketing
  • WhatsApp Business Policy compliance (anti-spam)

Recommendation: Legal consultation required; implement opt-in mechanisms and consent tracking

3. Google Sheets as Database

Using Google Sheets for production data has severe limitations:

  • API rate limits: 100 requests per 100 seconds per user
  • Slow performance (200-500ms per operation)
  • Does NOT scale beyond 1,000 rows
  • No transactional integrity (concurrent writes can corrupt data)
  • No proper indexing or query optimization

Recommendation: Use PostgreSQL database instead (essential for production)

4. Scope Creep Risk

The specification is 60+ pages with extensive feature lists mixing "must-have" and "nice-to-have" features without clear prioritization.

  • Risk of continuous requirement changes
  • Unclear definition of "done"
  • Potential for project timeline to extend indefinitely

Recommendation: Define clear MVP scope with locked requirements; any additions go through formal change request process

5. Technical Complexity - State Synchronization

Keeping state synchronized across multiple communication channels (email ↔ WhatsApp ↔ Voice ↔ LinkedIn) while handling:

  • 24 timezones + DST transitions
  • Race conditions (two people responding simultaneously)
  • Message ordering guarantees
  • Failure recovery and retry logic

Recommendation: Start with email-only to validate core logic before adding multi-channel complexity
